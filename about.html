About EcoLife

We help people live well without costing the Earth. Since 1997, EcoLife has connected communities to sustainable products, transparent data, and fair partnerships that protect people and the planet.

Our story Meet the team

Company history and background

EcoLife started in 1997 as a small collective partnering with local organic farms and fair‑trade co‑ops. Our mission was simple: make high‑quality sustainable goods accessible, verifiable, and affordable.

Over the years, we built long‑term relationships across the supply chain and introduced data‑backed impact tracking, ensuring that every claim we publish can be traced to real outcomes for people and ecosystems.
