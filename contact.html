FAQs

How long does shipping take?

Domestic orders arrive in 2–5 business days. International orders typically arrive in 7–14 business days.

Are your products certified?

Yes. We partner with certified suppliers (e.g., organic, fair trade) and list certifications on each product page.

Can I return or exchange items?

We offer a 30‑day return policy for unused items in original packaging. Start a return via your order confirmation email.

Do you ship plastic‑free?

Absolutely. All shipments use recyclable or compostable materials, with minimal ink and no plastic fillers.
